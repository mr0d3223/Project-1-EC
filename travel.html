My Favorite Adventures

Japan

More about my Japan trip

Out of all the countries I have the most time spent in Japan, living there for 2 years while I was in the Marine Corps and just having visited last New Years and with hopes of going again this New Years. It is a life changing experience and I highly recommend this being one of the must travel locations. The food is unrealistically amazing and is nothing but a movie moment in you life. The Culture is amazing, I had no clue that Japan has one of the best night lifes i have ever been to from basically going clubbing from sun down to sun up, amazing bars and street life vendors.

New Zealand

More about my New Zealand trip

New Zealand has to be one of the most beautiful places I have ever been. The scenery is something out of a movie with how unrealistic it beauty is. The climate is perfect for doing almost anything outdoors. This is where i went bungie jumping for the first time and it was into a beautiful crystal clear lake. This has to be one of my dream retirement locations for its amazing outdoor activities. Since there is no language barrier it makes so much easier to travel and enjoy the experience. I also got lucky enough to visit the set of Lord of the rings Hobbit town and it was an amazing experience!

Australia

More about my Australia trip

I was fortunate enough to visit Australia twice one on vacation and once during a work trip while in the Marine Corps. This is where I was lucky enough to go with a group of friends to Sydney and go sky diving for the first time. The beaches are beautiful even though I had a close encounter with a jellyfish I did not that discourage me from explore more. I also got to enjoy the nightlife here and was surprised as to how similar it is to LA nightlife.

Costa Rica

More about my Costa Rica trip

I was so beautiful being in Costa Rica, I went with a friend and the both of us had a blast doing snorkeling hikes, odoing tours of the indigenous people. It very much reminded me of my families home country of Nicaragua with many of the foods being similar. The warm tropical weather brought relaxation throughout my body. We picked a pretty remote small town to visit so we were able to do a lot in the 2 weeks we were down there.

Philippines

More about my Philippines trip

The Philippines was a whole experience. I initially went down there for my first combat deployment. So for the first 6 months that I was down there I was mainly working, but after my deployment finished we were allowed to explore more of Manila and that was a liberating experience after working like a dog with 16 hour shift. We did a lot of tours to famous spots to learn more about the culture. The food was amazing I even tried Balut which is a unique dish in the Phillipines, it was suprisingly good or at least the one I ate was good. There were so many concerts going on while I was down there and I even went to a night club that had Deadmau5 performing.

Bali

More about my Bali trip

Bali was so beautiful with its landscapes and activities that I had the opportunity to visit. Even going to a buddhist temple. The night life was amazing, my friends and I went out every night to a different club and tried all sort of new drinks some of which were on fire when served. This is also where I rode a motorcycle for the first time, and it was terrible I almost crashed with how crazy the driving is down there, but im still alive and would do it again.

Hawaii

More about my Hawaii trip

Hawaii is everything that everyone says about it! I am fortunate enough to have one of my Marine Corps buddies who lives there and because of that the last two times I went down there I didnt have to pay for lodging since him and his girlfriend let me stay with them. I took my buddy to go surfing for the first time, we both aced it the first few tries. I was able to go to Kualoa Ranch This is where they shoot almost all movies that have a tropical scene and i even did a horseback riding tour through the movie sets. My buddy and his girlfriend are huge ravers and they were able to get me into some big festivals down there for almost free and take me to many underground party scenes. I usually went on my own adventure to beaches or hiking when they went to work so it was a beautiful experience.
